So many in fact, that I have not been able to try them all.

The only drawback to having so many breweries is the lack of imported beer.




I am a huge fan of beer from Germany and Belgium.




All the breweries here try to emulate that style beer but none have gotten it right




I'm sure it has to do with the German Purity Law of 1516.




This law only allows German Breweries to brew with four ingredients in their beer.




Water, hops, barely, and yeast, which was later added.




America, let alone states, don't have a law where that is the requirement.




This brings about all the flavors in the beer, hence craft beer.
